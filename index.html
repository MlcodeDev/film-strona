INTRODUCTION

John Wick is a 2014 American Neo-noir action film that follows the story of a retired hitman - John Wick, who seeks vengeance against a group of criminals who killed his dog, a gift from his deceased wife. The film is known for its stylish action sequences and intricate fight choreography. The movie takes place in a dark and gritty world where assassins and crime lords operate in the shadows. The film's title character is a legendary hitman who is forced back into the game.

CAST

The film features Keanu Reeves as the titular character, John Wick. Ian McShane plays Winston, the manager of the Continental Hotel, a safe haven for assassins. Michael Nyquist plays Viggo Tarasov, a powerful crime lord who is the main antagonist of the film. Lance Reddick plays Charon, the concierge of the Continental Hotel. Alfie Allen, plays Iosef, a reckless and arrogant young man who kills John Wick's dog, sparking a chain of events that sets the plot of the film in motion.

RECEPTION AND IMPACT
